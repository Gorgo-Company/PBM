A Life of Purpose Built on Real Life

The whole woman, not one narrow title.

From a small beach town to a life of impact, Patrice's journey is one of love, legacy, loss, reinvention, and purpose. Her story is proof that it's never too late to choose you.

She is a coastal-town daughter, a wife of nearly four decades, a mother of grown children, a former mortgage professional, a writer, a public speaker, and a trusted coach for women navigating identity, money, menopause, grief, ambition, and the sacred work of beginning again.
